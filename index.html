Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Convertly collects minimal data to operate the service. We use browser cookies and local storage to track your daily free conversion usage limit (5 per day). We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you upgrade to Premium.

2. File Handling

All file conversions are processed client-side in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to our servers. Files processed locally are automatically cleared from memory when you close the tab.

  • Files are processed entirely in your browser
  • No file data is transmitted to external servers
  • No file content is stored, logged, or analyzed

3. Cookies & Local Storage

We use cookies and localStorage solely to track your free usage count. This data is stored on your device and expires daily. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's cookies and local storage.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts for typography (privacy policy at fonts.google.com). No analytics, advertising trackers, or third-party monitoring tools are embedded in the free version.

5. Security

Our website is served over HTTPS with SSL/TLS encryption. Since all file processing happens in your browser, your files never traverse the internet to our servers.
